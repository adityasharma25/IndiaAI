Transforming India's AI Ecosystem


IndiaAI is a national initiative aimed at fostering AI innovation and ensuring equitable access to AI resources. Through strategic
programs and partnerships, we empower startups, researchers, and institutions to excel in AI development.


In March 2024, the Government of India approved the IndiaAI Mission with the aim to build a precise and cohesive strategy for bridging the gaps in the existing AI ecosystem. The IndiaAI mission will establish a comprehensive ecosystem catalyzing AI innovation through strategic programs and partnerships across the public and private sectors. By democratizing computing access, improving data quality, developing indigenous AI capabilities, attracting top AI talent, enabling industry collaboration, providing startup risk capital, ensuring socially impactful AI projects and bolstering ethical AI, it will drive responsible, inclusive growth of India's AI ecosystem.
